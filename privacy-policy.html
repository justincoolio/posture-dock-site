Privacy Policy

Effective June 15, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Posture-Dock handles information when you use the Posture-Dock mobile application (the “App”). Posture-Dock is designed to operate without collecting your personal information.

Information We Collect

Posture-Dock does not collect personal information from you. No account creation, registration, or sign-in is required to use the App. You are not asked to provide your name, email address, phone number, physical address, or other identifying information.

Motion Sensor Data

The App uses motion sensor data made available by compatible AirPods to help calibrate your neutral posture and provide feedback during a posture session. This motion sensor data is processed locally on your device. It is not transmitted to the developer, uploaded to a server, or shared with third parties.

Session History

Posture session history is stored locally on your device so that you can review past sessions. Session history is not synced to a developer-operated cloud service and is not accessible to the developer. Deleting the App removes its locally stored data in accordance with iOS behavior.

Analytics, Tracking, and Advertising

Posture-Dock does not collect analytics or diagnostic usage data, does not use third-party tracking technologies, and does not include advertising networks. The App does not track you across apps or websites.

Sharing and Sale of Information

Because the developer does not collect personal information, there is no personal information to sell, rent, disclose, or share with third parties. Motion sensor data and session history remain on your device.

Apple Services

Apple may process information independently when you download, purchase, update, or use the App through Apple services. Apple’s handling of that information is governed by Apple’s own privacy policies and terms. Posture-Dock does not receive your Apple account credentials or payment information.

Data Security and Retention

Posture-Dock relies on the security protections provided by iOS for data stored locally by the App. Local session history remains on your device until you remove it through available App controls or delete the App. No method of electronic storage is guaranteed to be completely secure, but no App data is transmitted to or retained on developer-operated servers.

Children’s Privacy

Posture-Dock does not knowingly collect personal information from children or any other users. Since the App does not collect personal information, it does not maintain information that could be used to identify a child.

International Data Transfers

The developer does not transfer your motion sensor data, session history, or personal information between countries because this information is not collected by the developer.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated to reflect changes to the App or applicable requirements. When the policy changes, the effective date at the top of this page will be revised. Continued use of the App after an update means the updated policy applies to your use of the App.

Contact

For questions about this Privacy Policy, contact the developer using the support contact provided on the Posture-Dock App Store product page.
